Intelligent Safety & Performance Features

Donther is not just about speed and range; it’s also about smart, reliable safety features. Our e-bike is equipped with state-of-the-art auto temperature control technology. This system continuously monitors and regulates the bike’s temperature to prevent overheating. If temperatures exceed safe levels, the system automatically adjusts or powers down to protect both the rider and the bike. Additionally, our Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) ensures that you maintain control and stability even in sudden stops or adverse conditions, giving you a safer and more secure ride.
